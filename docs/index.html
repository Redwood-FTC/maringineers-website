About Us

Engineers at Heart

Maringineers is committed to inspiring the next generation of engineers and roboticists through hands-on learning and competition.

Our team focuses on creating innovative robotic solutions that blend engineering skills with teamwork. We are dedicated to nurturing students’ interests in robotics, coding, and engineering, preparing them for a successful future in technology.

Our Robotics Services

Maringineers offers a comprehensive suite of services dedicated to advancing robotics education. From our workshop to competitions, our services ensure hands-on learning experiences.

Our workshop (A.K.A., room 302) provide students with the tools and knowledge to build and program their own future projects, making learning both engaging and practical.

Mentorship Programs

Oportunities to lead the way for others

Our mentorship programs connect students with younger generations, offering insights and guidance to help them navigate their career paths in technology.

Competition Participation

Showcase Your Skills
